You Go Bees!

Bees are important

Bees are a living creature that can pollonate flowers to keep them alive. We need bees so we can have beautiful flowers, and honey. We also need them so they can fertilize crops.

Bees are endangered

Bees are facing a problem with people and pesticides. Gardeners spray pesticides on flower and othe plants so no bug eat them or ruins the crop. When they spray the pesticide they kill and bug or insect around it. This means if a bee lands on a flower that has been sprayed, it will obtain the poison and the bee will eventually die. This is called colony collapse disorder. We need to help them so they can do what they are suppose to do, make honry and pollinate. So how can we do ths?

You can help

Helping a bee can bee can be as easy as planting a flower. Bees love flowers. Me and my team are giving out seeds and taking donations to buy more seeds.

Please plant seends and donate to buy more seeds to help awareness of the Colony Collapse Disorder.
